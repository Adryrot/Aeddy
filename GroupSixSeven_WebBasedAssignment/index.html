What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method which developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late of 1980s. It uses a timer to break work into focused intervals, traditionally 25 minutes and separated by short breaks. This technique helps to improve focus, reduce mental fatigue and maintain consistent productivity throughout your study or work sessions.

Benefits: Enhanced concentration • Reduced burnout • Better time awareness • Improved work-life balance
